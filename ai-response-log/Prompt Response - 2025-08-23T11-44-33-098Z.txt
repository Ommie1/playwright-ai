What are UAE visa requirements?

Summary of UAE Visa Requirements (2025)

The visa requirements for entering the United Arab Emirates (UAE) depend on your nationality, the purpose of your visit, and the type of passport you hold. Below is a concise overview based on the latest available information:

1. Visa-Free Entry & Visa on Arrival
GCC Citizens : Citizens of Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries do not need a visa to enter the UAE.
Visa on Arrival : Nationals of certain countries can obtain a visa on arrival. The duration varies:
30-day visa on arrival : For citizens of specific countries, with a 10-day grace period for extension.
90-day visa on arrival : For citizens of other designated countries.
Special cases : Indian citizens with a valid US visa/green card (valid for at least 6 months) can get a 14-day visa on arrival.
Mexican citizens : Eligible for a 180-day multiple-entry visa.
Passport Validity : All visitors must have a passport valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry.
GCC Residence Permit Holders : As of April 29, 2016, must apply for a UAE visa before arrival.
Check Eligibility : Lists of eligible countries may change; always check with the UAE embassy or official websites before travel.
2
2. Tourist Visas
Who Needs It : Required for travelers not eligible for visa-free entry or visa on arrival.
How to Apply : Through UAE-based airlines, hotels, or licensed travel agents. The UAE embassies do not issue tourist visas directly.
Multiple Entry 5-Year Tourist Visa : Allows multiple entries, each stay up to 90 days (extendable for another 90 days). Available to all nationalities, subject to requirements such as proof of funds and health insurance.
Special Note : Females under 18 cannot apply unless traveling with parents. Children under 18 accompanied by adults can get a free visa from July 15 to September 15 each year.
4
3. Employment Visas
Who Needs It : Anyone wishing to work in the UAE.
Process Overview :
Employer applies for a quota approval.
Signed employment contract submitted to Ministry of Labour.
Entry permit and work visa application.
Medical testing and Emirates ID registration upon arrival.
Residence visa application and health insurance coverage.
Emirates ID issued (visa details now linked to ID, not stamped in passport).
Types :
Standard Employment Visa (sponsored by employer)
Green Visa (for skilled professionals, freelancers, investors)
Golden Visa (long-term, for investors, high-net-worth individuals)
5
4. General Requirements
Passport Validity : Minimum 6 months validity required.
Application Channels : Online via ICP and GDRFA websites/apps, or through registered typing centers.
Supporting Documents : Vary by visa type but generally include passport copy, photos, proof of accommodation, travel itinerary, and sometimes financial proof or health insurance.
3
5. Important Notes
Random Screening : Some travelers may be asked for a hard copy of their visa at Dubai International Airport.
Visa Validity & Extensions : Most entry permits are valid for 60 days; tourist visas and visit visas may be extended depending on type.
Always Check Latest Rules : Visa policies are subject to change; consult official UAE government or airline websites before travel.

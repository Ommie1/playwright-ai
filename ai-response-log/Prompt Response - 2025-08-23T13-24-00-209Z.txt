What are UAE visa requirements?

Summary of UAE Visa Requirements

The visa requirements for entering the United Arab Emirates (UAE) depend on your nationality, passport type, and the purpose of your visit. Below is a concise overview:

1. Visa-Free Entry
GCC Citizens : Citizens of Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries do not need a visa to enter the UAE.
Visa on Arrival : Many nationalities can obtain a visa on arrival for either 30 or 90 days, depending on their country of citizenship. For example:
30-day visa on arrival : Certain countriesâ€™ citizens receive a 30-day visa (with a 10-day grace period for extension) upon arrival.
90-day visa on arrival : Citizens of select countries receive a 90-day visa valid within a 180-day period.
U.S. Citizens : Receive a 90-day visa on arrival, valid for multiple entries within 180 days from first entry.
Indian Citizens : Eligible Indian nationals with a valid U.S. visa or Green Card (valid for at least 6 months) can obtain a 14-day visa on arrival (fee applies) 2 4 5.

Note: Passport must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry.

2. Pre-Arranged Visa
Tourist Visa : If you are not eligible for visa-free entry or visa on arrival, you must apply for a tourist visa before traveling. This can be arranged through:
UAE-based airlines
Licensed travel agents
Hotels in the UAE (if you book with them)
Multiple Entry 5-Year Tourist Visa : Available for all nationalities, allows multiple entries with up to 90 days per visit (extendable by another 90 days). Requires proof of funds, health insurance, and other documents 1.
3. Special Notes
GCC Residence Permit Holders : As of April 29, 2016, GCC residents (non-citizens) must apply for a UAE visa before arrival.
Children Under 18 : May be eligible for free visas during certain periods if traveling with adults.
Females Under 18 : Cannot apply for a tourist visa unless accompanied by parents.
4. Application Requirements
General Requirements :
Passport valid for at least 6 months
Completed visa application form
Passport-sized photographs
Proof of travel (flight tickets, hotel booking)
Additional documents may be required depending on visa type and nationality

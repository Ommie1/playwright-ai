What are UAE visa requirements?

Summary of UAE Visa Requirements (2025)

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) offers several types of visas, each with specific requirements depending on your nationality, purpose of visit, and length of stay. Below is a concise overview of the main visa categories and general requirements:

1. Visa-Free Entry & Visa on Arrival
GCC Citizens : Citizens of Gulf Cooperation Council countries do not require a visa to enter the UAE.
Visa on Arrival : Nationals from certain countries can obtain a visa on arrival, typically for 30 or 90 days, depending on their passport. For example:
30-day visa on arrival: Many countries, with a 10-day grace period for extension.
90-day visa on arrival: Select European and other countries.
Indian nationals with a valid US visa/green card or UK/EU residence permit can get a 14-day visa on arrival (fee applies).
Passport Validity : Passports must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry.
Check Eligibility : Lists of eligible countries may change; always check with the UAE embassy or official government portal before traveling.
Source: 7
2. Tourist Visas
Who Needs It : Required for travelers not eligible for visa-free entry or visa on arrival.
Application : Can be arranged through UAE-based airlines, hotels, or licensed travel agents. UAE embassies do not issue tourist visas directly.
Multiple Entry 5-Year Tourist Visa : Available for all nationalities, allows multiple entries with up to 90 days per visit (extendable).
Documents Required : Passport (6 months validity), passport-size photo, proof of funds, travel insurance, and sometimes hotel booking or return ticket.
Special Note : Females under 18 must travel with parents to be eligible.
Source: 6
3. Employment Visas
Who Needs It : Anyone seeking to work in the UAE.
Process Overview :
Employer applies for quota approval from Ministry of Labour.
Signed employment contract submitted for approval.
Employer applies for entry permit and work visa.
Medical testing and Emirates ID registration upon arrival.
Residence visa application and health insurance provided by employer.
Emirates ID issued; visa details now linked to Emirates ID (no passport stamping required).
Medical Tests : Screening for infectious diseases is mandatory.
Health Insurance : Employers must provide basic health insurance.
Source: 5
4. Long-Term Residency Visas
Green Visa : For skilled professionals, freelancers, investors, and entrepreneurs.
Golden Visa : 10-year residency for investors, entrepreneurs, and certain professionals.
Eligibility : Varies by category; typically requires proof of investment, professional status, or specialized skills.
Source: 5
5. General Entry Requirements
Passport Validity : Minimum 6 months validity required for all visitors.
Entry Permit : Most entry permits are valid for 60 days; must finalize residence visa procedures within this period.
Application Channels : Online via ICP and GDRFA websites/apps, or through registered typing centers.
Source: 4
6. Important Notes
Overstaying : Penalties apply for overstaying your visa.
Visa Cancellation : All visas must be formally cancelled before a new one can be issued.
Legal Compliance : Strict adherence to UAE laws and customs is required; violations can result in fines, imprisonment, or deportation.
Special Restrictions : Some medications, behaviors, and items are prohibited—check official guidance before travel.
Source: 3

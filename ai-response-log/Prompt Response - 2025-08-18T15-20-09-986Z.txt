What are UAE visa requirements?

UAE Visa Requirements (2025)

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) has a range of visa requirements depending on your nationality, the purpose of your visit, and the length of your stay. Below is a structured overview of the main visa categories and their requirements:

1. Visa-Free Entry & Visa on Arrival
Visa-Free/On Arrival : Citizens of certain countries (e.g., EU, US, UK, Australia, Japan, South Korea, and others) do not need to arrange a visa in advance. They receive a visa on arrival at the airport.
30-day visa on arrival: For many nationalities, valid for 30 days, extendable once for a fee.
90-day visa on arrival: For select European and other countries, valid for 90 days within a 180-day period.
Indian nationals with a valid US visa or Green Card, UK or EU residency, can get a 14-day visa on arrival (USD 63 fee, extendable once).
Passport must be valid for at least six months from the date of entry.
Check your eligibility using the UAE government or Emirates airline visa tool.
GCC (Gulf Cooperation Council) residents must now apply for a UAE visa before arrival.
For a full list of eligible countries and details, see: 2
2. Tourist Visa
Who needs it? : Travelers not eligible for visa-free entry or visa on arrival.
Application channels : UAE-based airlines, hotels, licensed travel agents, or through the official government portals.
Multiple Entry 5-Year Tourist Visa : Allows multiple entries, up to 90 days per visit (extendable for another 90 days), for all nationalities.
Requirements include proof of funds, health insurance, and a passport valid for at least six months.
Females under 18 must travel with parents.
UAE embassies do not issue tourist visas directly.
More info: 1
3. Employment/Residence Visa
Who needs it? : Anyone intending to work or reside in the UAE.
Process :
Employer applies for a work permit and entry visa.
Employee enters UAE on entry permit (valid for 60 days).
Undergoes medical testing and applies for Emirates ID.
Employer applies for residence visa.
Health insurance is mandatory.
Visa details are now linked to the Emirates ID (no passport stamping required).
Special categories :
Green Visa: For skilled professionals, freelancers, investors, and entrepreneurs.
Golden Visa: 10-year residency for investors, scientists, exceptional talents, etc.
Key documents : Valid passport (6+ months), employment contract, educational certificates, medical clearance, and insurance.
More details : 3
4. General Entry Requirements
Passport must be valid for at least six months from the date of entry.
One blank page required for entry stamp.
Some nationalities may need to apply for a pre-arranged visa before travel.
All travelers must exit the UAE with the passport used for entry.
Overstaying your visa can result in fines or legal issues.
Certain medications, items, and behaviors are strictly regulated—check UAE customs and legal guidelines before travel.
For official information and updates: 4
5. Where to Apply
Online via:
ICP (Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship) and GDRFA (General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs) websites and apps.
Typing centers registered with ICP or GDRFA.
Airlines, hotels, and travel agencies for tourist visas.
For more details: 5
6. Important Notes
UAE embassies do not issue tourist visas.
Always check the latest requirements as rules may change.
Ensure all documents are genuine and meet UAE standards.
Working on a tourist or visit visa is strictly prohibited.
For the most accurate and up-to-date requirements, always consult the official UAE government portal or your airline before travel.
